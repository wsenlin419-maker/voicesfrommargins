Voices from Margins

Initiative

Voices from Margins is an independent, nonpartisan research platform and fact tank that explores the intersections of language, identity, inequality, and visual politics. It aims to bridge academic rigor—such as quantitative visual analysis and deep ethnographic fieldwork—with compelling public narratives.

The center conducts multimodal content analysis, sociolinguistic demographic research, and in-depth ethnographic interviews. We study global geopolitical narratives and local linguistic hybridity to provide data-driven insights.
